# Is Reading Always Beneficial?

## About

This dictation is designed for **B2 level** English learners.

## Video

[![Watch on YouTube](https://img.shields.io/badge/▶_Watch_on_YouTube-red?style=for-the-badge&logo=youtube)](https://youtu.be/SYihTCG_9Xk)

This video is designed for B2 level English learners.
You will first listen to the full text, then practice sentence shadowing, and finally repeat the text at full speed.

## How to Use

1. Watch the video to hear the full text and get familiar with the content.
2. Open the [Dictation App](https://leva13007.github.io/dictation_app/?dic=0002) and practice typing what you hear sentence by sentence.
3. Use hints if needed and replay sentences at different speeds.
