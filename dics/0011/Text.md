# Complaint About School Dance Catering Service
Dear Sir or Madam,
I am writing on behalf of the school dance committee to express our disappointment with the service your company provided at our recent school dance last Friday.
To begin with, the staff who attended the event appeared inexperienced and unprofessional.
Most of them seemed to be teenagers, they arrived late, and several members of staff were rather rude to guests.
Furthermore, drinks stopped being served at midnight, even though the dance continued for another two hours.
In addition, we were dissatisfied with the entertainment.
Your advertisement promised a professional DJ suitable for teenage audiences; however, he played only music from the 1950s throughout the entire evening.
As a result, many students left the dance floor early.
The food was also far from satisfactory.
Instead of the “gourmet buffet” advertised, guests were served only hot dogs and cheese pies.
To make matters worse, the free celebration cake included in the package was never provided.
Finally, the marquee took almost four hours to set up, which meant that the event started one hour late.
Considering all the above, I believe we are entitled to a partial refund.
I look forward to your prompt response.
Yours faithfully,
John Smith
President of the School Dance Committee
